## Cross-Cutting Themes

### 1. Risk Management is Priority #1
**Supporters:** Dennis, Seykota, Hite, Kovner, Jones, O'Neil, Schwartz
> Never risk more than 1-2% per trade. Cut losses fast. The single most agreed-upon rule.

### 2. Trend Following Works
**Supporters:** Dennis, Seykota, Hite, Kovner, Jones
> Following established trends is the most reliable edge.

### 3. Let Winners Run
**Supporters:** Dennis, Seykota, Hite, Marcus, Kovner
> A few big winners pay for many small losers. Don't cut winners short.

### 4. Reduce Size During Drawdowns
**Supporters:** Jones, Schwartz, Marcus
> When losing, reduce position size. Protect capital for the next opportunity.

### 5. Moving Averages as Core Indicator
**Supporters:** Seykota, Schwartz, Hite, O'Neil
> MA crossovers are the most commonly used systematic signal across these traders.
